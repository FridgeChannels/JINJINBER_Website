# Other Valve product details (Enoch Industry)

## 1) Hydraulic Control Valve
**Specifications**
- Nominal Pressure: PN10, 16, 25, 40.
- Nominal Diameter: DN15 ~ DN1000, and even larger.
- Body Materials: Gray Cast Iron (HT250), Ductile Iron (QT450-10), Cast Steel (WCB), Stainless Steel (CF8/304, CF8M/316), Copper Alloy, etc.
- Connections: Flange, Threaded.

**Product description**
A hydraulic control valve uses the pressure, flow rate, or liquid level of the medium (water) in a pipeline as a power source. Through a pre-set pilot valve and control system, it automatically opens, closes, or regulates the main valve.

**Product function**
Remote control switch function / precise adjustment function

- In large or complex piping systems with valves in inconvenient locations (heights, underground wells), a pilot valve can be installed in a convenient location (e.g., control room) to remotely open/close the main pipeline valve.
- Stabilizes parameters such as pressure and flow within the required range according to set values; if parameters deviate, it automatically adjusts valve opening to restore stability.

**Value advantage**
- Highly automated, no external power required
- Functional integration, multi-purpose valve
- Smooth operation and high reliability
- Improved system safety and stability

**Application scenario**
- Building water supply and drainage systems: domestic water supply and fire water supply for high-rise buildings, water tank level control, zoned pressure reduction.
- Fire protection systems: pressure stabilization, pressure reduction, water hammer prevention at fire pump outlets, system overpressure relief.
- Water conservancy and municipal water supply: flow, water level control, pressure regulation, safety pressure relief in reservoirs, water plants, and water pipelines.
- Industrial circulation systems: pressure and flow control for chilled water and cooling water systems in central air conditioning.
- Irrigation systems: zoned control and pressure management for large agricultural irrigation projects.

## 2) Safety Valve
**Specifications**
- Nominal Diameter: DN50, DN100, etc.
- Connection Type: Flange, Threaded, Welded
- Valve Body Material: Carbon Steel (WCB), Stainless Steel (CF8/304, CF8M/316), Alloy Steel (WC6, WC9), etc.
- Applicable Media: Steam, Air/Gas, Liquids (Water, Oil), etc.

**Product description**
A safety valve is an automatic valve that uses the pressure of the medium itself to discharge a certain amount of fluid without any external force, preventing internal system pressure from exceeding a predetermined safety value. Once pressure returns to normal, it automatically closes to stop further flow.

**Product function**
Overpressure relief / system protection / life and property safety

- When system pressure abnormally rises (cooling failure, runaway reaction, fire, misoperation, etc.), the safety valve acts as the last defense, releasing medium and forcing pressure within a safe range.
- Prevents overpressure to protect pressure vessels, pipelines, boilers, heat exchangers, avoiding damage/scrapping or costly repairs.
- Prevents catastrophic energy release from ruptures/explosions that could cause casualties, property loss, environmental disasters—directly protects operators, public, and facilities.

**Value advantage**
- Fully automated and fast response
- High reliability
- Large discharge capacity
- Wide range of applications

**Application scenario**
- Energy and power: boilers, pressure vessels, steam pipes, steam turbines.
- Petrochemical: reactors, distillation towers, storage tanks, LPG spherical tanks, long-distance pipelines.
- Pharmaceutical and food: fermentation tanks, autoclaves, food processing equipment.
- Other industries: air compressors, refrigeration systems, hydraulic systems, etc.
