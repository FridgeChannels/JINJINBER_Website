Butterfly Valve 产品介绍信息汇总

----------------------------------------
1) Wafer-Type Butterfly Valve
- Nominal Pressure: PN6, 10, 16, 25.
- Nominal Diameter: DN 50 to DN 2000, and even larger.
- Body Materials: Gray Cast Iron (HT250), Ductile Iron (QT400), Cast Steel (WCB), Stainless Steel (CF8/304, CF8M/316, CF3/304L, CF3M/316L), Duplex Steel, etc.
- Connection Type: Wafer-Type.

## Product description
The wafer-type butterfly valve has no flanges on its body. Instead, it is clamped directly between two pipe flanges using a few bolts threaded through holes in the valve body. Its key features are short length, light weight, and low cost.

## Product function
Connecting and Cutting Off; Flow Regulation.
Fully open/close by rotating the butterfly plate 90°. Fully open: plate parallel to fluid direction, flow path approximately straight. Fully closed: plate perpendicular, sealing ring presses against valve seat to seal and cut off medium. The opening can stop anywhere between 0° and 90° to control flow rate/pressure.

## Value advantage
- Compact structure and light weight
- Low cost and good economic efficiency
- Quick opening and closing, easy operation
- Low fluid resistance

## Application scenario
- Water supply and drainage systems: municipal water supply, sewage treatment, fire protection systems
- Heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC): chilled water, hot water, cooling water systems
- Chemical industry: various non-corrosive chemical liquids
- Power: circulating water and cooling water systems
- Food and pharmaceutical: sanitary designs used for material handling
- Shipbuilding: seawater, ballast water, bilge water systems
- Compressed Air and Gas: low-pressure air and gas pipelines

2) Lug Butterfly Valve
- Nominal Pressure: PN6, 10, 16, 25.
- Nominal Diameter: DN50 ~ DN1200 and larger.
- Body Materials: Gray Cast Iron (HT250), Ductile Iron (QT450), Cast Steel (WCB), Stainless Steel (CF8/304, CF8M/316), Copper Alloy.
- Connection Type: Double-lug wafer-type.

## Product description
A lug butterfly valve is a wafer-type butterfly valve with bolt-hole lugs on both ends of the valve body. Bolts pass directly through these lugs to secure the valve between two pipe flanges without passing through the valve interior.

## Product function
Shutdown and Sealing; Flow Regulation; Bidirectional Flow Blocking.
Fully open/close by rotating the butterfly plate 90°. Closed state: plate perpendicular, blocking flow; valve seat and plate edge seal tightly to ensure minimal leakage. Opening angle can be controlled anywhere between 0° and 90° to regulate flow. Standard design allows pressure from either direction when closed and effective sealing when pressure released from one side.

## Value advantage
- Easy installation, especially suitable for terminal equipment
- Compact structure and light weight
- Highly cost-effective
- Low flow resistance and fast opening/closing

## Application scenario
- Water supply and drainage systems: municipal water supply, sewage treatment plants, fire protection systems
- HVAC: hot/cold water circulation systems
- Industrial processes: on/off control of non-corrosive media (chemical, food, pharmaceutical, paper industries)
- Shipbuilding: seawater and ballast water systems
- Energy industry: power plant cooling water systems

3) D71FX Butterfly Valve
- Nominal Pressure: PN 6, 10, 16 (fluoroplastic lining generally not resistant to high pressures).
- Nominal Diameter: DN50 ~ DN300, and even larger.
- Applicable Temperature: generally -20°C to +150°C (depending on fluoroplastic type; PTFE can withstand higher).
- Applicable Media: acids, alkalis, salts, organic solvents and other highly corrosive media (e.g., sulfuric acid, hydrochloric acid, nitric acid, sodium hydroxide, brine, organic solvents).
- Leakage Rating: generally zero leakage or Class VI / Class V sealing as per ANSI B16.104.

## Product description
D71FX is a manually operated wafer-type butterfly valve with internal contact areas lined with fluoroplastic.
- D: butterfly valve
- 7: manual actuation
- 1: wafer-type connection
- F: fluoroplastic lining/sealing
- X: disc is rubber

## Product function
Connecting or Cutting Off the Medium; Flow Adjustment.
Rotating handle 90° opens/closes pipeline; fully open: plate parallel with low flow resistance. Fully closed: PTFE soft sealing ring fits tightly against seat to seal and prevent leakage. Handle position between 0° and 90° changes opening to adjust flow/pressure.

## Value advantage
- Excellent corrosion resistance
- Excellent sealing performance
- Low fluid resistance, energy saving
- Easy and flexible operation

## Application scenario
- Chemical Industry: process piping for acid/alkali/salt production lines
- Pharmaceutical: transport of corrosive materials in API production
- Pesticide Industry: corrosive chemicals
- Non-ferrous metallurgy: electrolyte and acid systems in hydrometallurgy
- Environmental Protection: wastewater treatment, acid mist purification systems
- Petrochemical: auxiliary pipelines for some corrosive media

4) D371 X Butterfly Valve
- Nominal Pressure: PN6, 10, 16.
- Nominal Diameter: DN50 ~ DN1200 and larger.
- Applicable Temperature: depends on seat seal material, typically -20°C to +120°C (rubber seals); PTFE seals may handle other ranges.
- Applicable Media: water, sewage, air, steam, oil, gas, corrosive and non-corrosive fluids depending on body and seal materials.
- Leakage Level: soft-sealed butterfly valves can generally meet “zero leakage” (ANSI Class VI).

## Product description
D371 butterfly valve is a worm gear drive, wafer-type, centerline butterfly valve.
- D: butterfly valve
- 3: worm gear drive
- 7: wafer-type connection
- 1: centerline structure

## Product function
Cutting Off and Connecting the Medium; Flow Regulation.
Rotating worm gear turns stem and plate 90° to fully open/close the pipeline; quick operation suits switch valve. Worm gear angle between 0° and 90° changes opening to regulate flow/pressure.

## Value advantage
- Compact structure, lightweight, space-saving
- Quick opening and closing, easy operation
- Low fluid resistance, energy saving
- Excellent sealing performance

## Application scenario
- Water supply/drainage and wastewater treatment: urban networks, pumping stations, sewage treatment
- HVAC: hot/cold water, cooling water circulation
- Energy and power: power plant cooling and circulating water systems
- Petrochemical: low corrosion process piping
- Metallurgy, papermaking, pharmaceuticals and other industrial sectors

5) Pot-Groove Clamp-Type Butterfly Valve
- Nominal Pressure: PN10, 16, 25.
- Nominal Diameter: DN50 ~ DN600 (2" ~ 24").
- Body Material: Ductile Iron (common), Cast Steel, Stainless Steel (304, 316), Copper, etc.
- Seal Material: Nitrile (NBR), EPDM, etc.

## Product description
A pot-groove clamp-type butterfly valve combines grooved (clamp) connection features with a wafer-type butterfly valve body. It lacks flanges and is clamped between two pipes using clamps.

## Product function
Connecting and Cutting Off Media; Flow Regulation.
Rotating handle/turbine/actuator rotates plate within 90° to fully open/close pipeline controlling flow (water, air, oil). Fixing plate at intermediate angle changes opening area for coarse flow adjustment.

## Value advantage
- Extremely easy and fast installation
- Compact structure, space-saving
- Easy maintenance and replacement
- Suitable for certain pipes/piping systems

## Application scenario
- Fire sprinkler systems
- Water supply and drainage: municipal, building water, sewage treatment
- HVAC: hot/cold water circulation systems
- Industrial piping: food, beverage, pharmaceutical (frequent cleaning/maintenance), plus mining and power plant pipelines
